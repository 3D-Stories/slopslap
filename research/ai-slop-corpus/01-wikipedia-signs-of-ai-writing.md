## Undue emphasis on significance, legacy, broader trends  → slopslap: emptiness / inflated symbolism

**Before (Statistical Institute of Catalonia, Sept 2024 revision):**
> "The Statistical Institute of Catalonia was officially established in 1989, marking a pivotal moment in the evolution of regional statistics in Spain. [...] The founding of Idescat represented a significant shift toward regional statistical independence... This initiative was part of a broader movement across Spain to decentralize administrative functions and enhance regional governance."

**Before (Deadbot, Oct 2025):**
> "GriefBots have prompted broader reflection on mortality and memory in a digital age. They blur boundaries between life and data, raising philosophical questions about identity, authenticity, and what it means to 'live on' through algorithms."
> *(page note: each sentence follows the rule of three; last uses curly quotes)*

**Before (Bacnotan, Dec 2024):**
> "This etymology highlights the enduring legacy of the community's resistance and the transformative power of unity in shaping its identity."

**Before (Lethrinops lethrinus, July 2024 — biology over-emphasis on ecosystem/conservation):**
> "However, the general health of the Lake Malawi ecosystem is crucial for the survival of this and other endemic species. Factors such as overfishing, pollution, and habitat destruction could potentially impact their populations."

Tells: inflated significance, "pivotal moment," "broader movement," "enhance," forced ecosystem/conservation framing.

## Canned emphasis on notability, attribution, media coverage → slopslap: laundering / emptiness

**Before (Sinead Bovell, Feb 2025):**
> "She spoke about AI on CNN, and was featured in Vogue, Wired, Toronto Star, and other media. [...] Her insights have also been featured in *Wired*, *Refinery29*, and other prominent media outlets."

**Before (AfC Help desk, Nov 2025 — echoes Wikipedia's own notability guideline wording):**
> "The subject has been profiled in multiple high-quality, independent, and widely-read outlets, including The Australian, SBS News, 7News... These sources provide significant, substantial, secondary coverage, not trivial mentions or press releases."

**Before (Forum Mall Kochi, June 2025 — "active social media presence" idiom):**
> "The mall maintains a strong digital presence, particularly on Instagram... Forum Kochi has consistently demonstrated excellence in digital promotions, with high-quality, engaging, and impactful video content..."

Tells: listing outlets as proof of notability, "maintains an active/strong ... presence," guideline-echoing.

## Superficial analyses (trailing -ing participles) → slopslap: emptiness / simulation

**Before (Douéra, June 2023):**
> "...the population of Douera stood at approximately 56,998 inhabitants, creating a lively community within its borders. Situated in the central-north region... Douera enjoys close proximity to the capital city, Algiers, further enhancing its significance as a dynamic hub of activity and culture."

**Before (McAllen Texas Temple, Nov 2024 — inflated symbolism + -ing):**
> "The temple's color palette of blue, green, and gold resonates with the region's natural beauty, symbolizing Texas bluebonnets, the Gulf of Mexico, and the diverse Texan landscapes. These colors and patterns evoke enduring faith and resilience, qualities that resonate deeply within this close-knit, cross-border community."

**Before (Darbhanga Junction, Aug 2024):**
> "It holds a pivotal place in the East Central Railway Zone... serving as a major railway hub with historical significance. [...] facilitating the movement of passengers and goods... contributing to the socio-economic development of the region... reflecting its continued relevance in the regional and national transportation landscape."

Tells: "-ing" clauses tacked on for fake depth ("symbolizing," "reflecting," "contributing to," "enhancing").

## Promotional / advertisement-like language → slopslap: emptiness / promotional

**Before (Alamata woreda, June 2023):**
> "Nestled within the breathtaking region of Gonder in Ethiopia, Alamata Raya Kobo stands as a vibrant town with a rich cultural heritage... From its scenic landscapes to its historical landmarks, Alamata Raya Kobo offers visitors a fascinating glimpse into the diverse tapestry of Ethiopia."

**Before (Cadillac Sollei, April 2025):**
> "The SOLLEI's exterior design communicates a powerful emotional presence... Smooth, uninterrupted surfaces and a pronounced A-line accentuate the vehicle's overall length, while a sleek, low tail imparts a sense of refined dynamism... showcasing the brand's dedication to craftsmanship and historical reverence."

Tells: "nestled," "breathtaking," "vibrant," "rich," "stands as," travel-brochure/press-release tone.

## Vague attributions & overgeneralized opinions → slopslap: laundering (convert to a question, never delete)

**Before (Haolai River, June 2025):**
> "Due to its unique characteristics, the Haolai River is of interest to researchers and conservationists. Efforts are ongoing to monitor its ecological health..."

**Before (BRICS, July 2025 — one source presented as consensus):**
> "While Pakistan was not directly named, the reference to cross-border terrorism, according to Indian sources, was widely interpreted as aimed at Islamabad."

Tells: "researchers and conservationists" (unnamed), "widely interpreted," "experts argue," inflating one cited source into many.

## Outline-like "Challenges and Future Prospects" → slopslap: emptiness

**Before (Korattur, April 2024):**
> "Despite its industrial and residential prosperity, Korattur faces challenges typical of urban areas, including [...] With its strategic location and ongoing initiatives, Korattur continues to thrive as an integral part of the Ambattur industrial zone, embodying the synergy between industry and residential living."

**Before (Panama Canal, March 2025):**
> "Despite its success, the Panama Canal faces challenges, including [...] Future investments in technology, such as automated navigation systems, and potential further expansions could enhance the canal's efficiency and maintain its relevance in global trade."

Tell: rigid "Despite [promo], X faces challenges... Despite these challenges, X continues to thrive."

## High density of "AI vocabulary" → slopslap: lexical_structural (candidate-selection aid only)

**Words-to-watch list (verbatim):** Additionally, align with, boasts, bolstered, crucial, delve, emphasizing, enduring, enhance, fostering, garner, highlight (verb), interplay, intricate/intricacies, key (adj), landscape (abstract), meticulous/meticulously, pivotal, robust, showcase, tapestry (abstract), testament, underscore (verb), valuable, vibrant.

**Era breakdown (verbatim, useful for dating a draft):**
- 2023–mid-2024 (GPT-4): Additionally, boasts, bolstered, crucial, delve, emphasizing, enduring, garner, intricate, interplay, key, landscape, meticulous, pivotal, underscore, tapestry, testament, valuable, vibrant
- mid-2024–mid-2025 (GPT-4o): align with, bolstered, crucial, emphasizing, enhance, enduring, fostering, highlighting, pivotal, showcasing, underscore, vibrant
- mid-2025+ (GPT-5): emphasizing, enhance, highlighting, showcasing (+ notability-cluster words)
- Grok idiosyncratic: causal, empirical, correlate, underscore (still, as of 2026)

This culinary tapestry is a direct result of Somalia's longstanding heritage of vibrant trade... An enduring testament to the influence of Italian colonial rule... showcasing how these dishes have integrated..."

## ★ Avoidance of basic copulatives ("is"/"are") → slopslap: lexical_structural — TWO REAL BEFORE→AFTER DIFFS

**★ Gallery 825 (Los Angeles Art Association, Aug 2023 revision — the AFTER is the AI edit, worse):**
- **Human original (−):** "Gallery 825 on La Cienega Boulevard, which was purchased in 1958, **is** LAAA's exhibition arm for contemporary art. There are four individual gallery spaces..."
- **AI copyedit (+):** "Gallery 825 on La Cienega Boulevard **serves as** LAAA's exhibition space for contemporary art. The gallery **features** four separate spaces..."
- *For slopslap the desired direction is the reverse: restore "is"/"has".*

**★ Harian Metro (Nov 2024 revision):**
- **Human original (−):** "It **is** Malaysia's first Malay daily afternoon Tabloid... The Harian Metro **was established** in March 1991 and **is** the first and oldest Malay-language tabloid..."
- **AI copyedit (+):** "It **was established** in March 1991 as Malaysia's first Malay-language afternoon tabloid... Harian Metro **holds the distinction of being** the first and oldest..."

Tell: "serves as / stands as / marks / represents," "features/offers/boasts/maintains," "holds the distinction of being," "refers to" instead of "is."

## Negative parallelisms → slopslap: synthetic cadence ("X, not Y") — HIGH PRIORITY for slopslap

### "Not just X, but also Y"
**Before (Talk:Eric Dick, Aug 2024):** "This choice of language is not only dismissive but also unnecessarily harsh and confrontational. [...] These comments are not just dismissive—they're outright disrespectful."
**Before (Self-portrait Kusama, April 2025):** "...constitutes not only a work of self-representation, but a visual document of her obsessions, visual strategies and psychobiographical narratives."

### "Not X, but Y"
**Before (Self-portrait Kusama, May 2025):** "This dispersal is not dissolution. Rather, it constitutes what Deleuze might describe as 'becoming'—an identity in flux... Kusama's self-portrait is not a mirror but a portal: not a representation of self, but a mechanism for its constant reinvention."
**Before (AfD:Lilly Contino, June 2025):** "Not a career, not a body of work, not sustained relevance — just an algorithmic moment."

### "X rather than Y" (Grok-common)
**Before (First Battle of Guilin, April 2026, from Grokipedia):** "...prioritizing empirical consolidation of power amid fragmented loyalties rather than ideological purity."

Tell: the contrastive frame as a rhythm crutch. slopslap flags this as `X, not Y` cadence (the kukakuka PRD had ×16 of these).

## Meta / detection caveats worth carrying into slopslap's discipline

- "Do not solely rely on AI-detection tools (e.g. GPTZero)... non-trivial error rates."
- 2025 study: human ability to distinguish LLM vs human text is "no better than random chance."
- "human speech and writing is being influenced by LLMs, and thus they are becoming more similar."
- These directly support slopslap's keystone rule (a stylistic feature is not itself harm / not proof of authorship).
